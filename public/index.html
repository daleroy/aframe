About A-Frame Construction

Dave Konezny

Owner


Founded in 2003. Dave Konezny is president and owner of A-Frame Construction, a general contracting company in San Diego that specializes in custom homes, remodels and additions in the greater San Diego area. Dave has over 25 years of experience in the construction industry, as well as an extensive background in custom carpentry, concrete and framing. Dave moved to San Diego on a mission in 1999 – to become a successful general contractor in Southern California. Dave has had a lifelong interest in building and began at a young age helping at his uncle’s general contracting business. Dave puts his experience and passion to work for clients, cultivating trust in his company’s ability to complete every job with client satisfaction as a top priority. His commitment to quality craftsmanship, honesty, integrity, reliability and responsiveness have enabled Dave to build A-Frame Construction almost exclusively on referrals from past or current clients. As a working contractor, Dave can be found in the field each day constructing and managing each and every project. His hands-on approach combined with his unique skills as a builder provide his clients with an unmatched level of service and quality.
